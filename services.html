SUCCESS IN SCHOOL USING SENSORY STRATEGIES

Part A of this is a hands-on workshop focuses on providing teachers and educators a foundational understanding of sensory systems and how they affect kids throughout the day. We examine the 7 sensory systems to gain a better understanding of how sensory input affects a child’s behavior. This workshop provides sensory strategies for working with over reactive and under reactive kids and promotes a bottom up approach on how to provide accommodations and modifications to the environment and give teachers and educators tools and strategies to help bring kids to a ready learning state. Pat B zeroes in on the importance of movement and review’s specific movements to incorporate throughout the day as well as integrating movement more into the learning environment. Given the fundamental knowledge of sensory systems and how movement plays such an important role educators are empowered to use the resources around them and gain a better insight into certain behaviors.
